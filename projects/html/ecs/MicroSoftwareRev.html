Microsoft Software Availability


The California State University system has reached a four-year licensing

agreement with Microsoft and Wareforce, our distributor of Microsoft

software. The terms of the agreement provides 30,000 licenses for full and

part-time faculty and staff, and 180,000 student licenses. Licensing during this

period includes both current and future versions of the covered software. The

agreement extends mostly for on-campus use of the software,but it may be

installed on home and laptop systems if at least 80% of ts use is university-

related. Additional information regarding the CSU/Microsoft Agreement can be

found at the following sites:

  • San Jose State University
 Computing at SJSU
  • California State University
CSU-Microsoft Agreement
  • Wareforce                      
 CSU page at Wareforce


SJSU students wishing to take advantage of the Agreement

should contact the Spartan Shop to obtain licenses and borrow

Microsoft CDs for a small administrative charge. A refundable

deposit is also required for each CD checked out.

More informaton is available at the SJSU Spartan Shop website:
http://www.spartanshops.com/MSCSU.html

Faculty and staff who qualify can order software and accompanying manuals at

a discounted price through the following means:

  • SJSU purchasing department with a Fast Track purchase requisition form.

  • The CSU page at Wareforce: http://www.wareforce.com/customer/csu/csu.html
  • Contact your COE departmental technical support representative to install the software on your system.
    No software documentation is available under this option.
  • Fill out an ECSJob Request Form.
    Software is loaned out for a period of 24 hours from Monday through Thursday, and through the weekend if checked out on Friday. No software documentation is available under this option.
