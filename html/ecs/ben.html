Equipment Systems Specialist

The Equipment Systems Specialist provides hardware support and maintenance for the College of Engineering.

 The responsibilities of the Equipment Systems Specialist include:

  • Maintains, installs, repairs, and troubleshoots component level hardware in microcomputer systems, peripheral equipment, and local area networks.

  • Provides technical support for faculty and staff.

  • Acts as liaison with hardware and software vendors.

  • Recommends upgrade requirements for software and hardware.

  • Maintains and monitors the College of Engineering computer inventory.

  • Trains and works closely with student assistants.
