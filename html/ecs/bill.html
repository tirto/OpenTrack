Network Administrator

The Network Administrator is responsible for the design, installation, maintenance, and management of the local area network (LAN) within the College of Engineering domain (engr.sjsu.edu). The Engineering domain also includes the satellite buildings of Aviation, located at the SJSU Airport, and the Industrial Studies Building located adjacent the College of Engineering Building.

In particular, the Network administrator's primary responsibilities are:

  • Develop and install network topology changes in the LAN to meet changing requirements.
  • Evaluate and select LAN equipment.
  • Upgrade and optimize existing LAN equipment.
  • Provide hardware and software support for connections to the LAN.
  • Provide LAN services such as Domain Name Services (DNS), email, network monitoring, and printing.
  • Assist faculty, staff, and department technicians with network operating system software and hardware related problems.


The Network Administrator also works closely with other campus technical staff, especially the central computing office of the CIO, in formulating network decisions which can potentially impact the College of Engineering.
